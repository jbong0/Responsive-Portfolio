About Me

How to paint. That's easy. What to paint. That's much harder. If I paint something, I don't want to have to explain what it is. What the devil.

Maybe we got a few little happy bushes here, just covered with snow. Let's do that again. Isn't it great to do something you can't fail at? You better get your coat out, this is going to be a cold painting.

How do you make a round circle with a square knife? That's your challenge for the day. Van Dyke Brown is a very nice brown, it's almost like a chocolate brown. Just let go - and fall like a little waterfall. You have to make almighty decisions when you're the creator. That's the way I look when I get home late; black and blue.
